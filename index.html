THE NAME: ELFEKI

From Arabic faqīh (فقيه) — jurist, scholar, expert in Islamic jurisprudence. Not a random surname but a hereditary occupational title. The Elfeki family produced judges, religious scholars, and community leaders across centuries. 78% of all Elfekis worldwide reside in North Africa. The name persists in Egypt (1,495), Saudi Arabia (358), Tunisia, and across the Mediterranean diaspora. When the Indian merchant married into the line of faqīhs, two forms of intelligence merged: the trader's commercial genius and the scholar's analytical rigor.

Source: Forebears.io, MyHeritage, ALFRED Database — Global surname incidence: ~1,931 bearers worldwide

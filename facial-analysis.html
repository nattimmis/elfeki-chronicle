Methodology Note: This analysis employs Phenotype-to-Genotype Reverse Inference (PGRI), a comparative morphological approach that estimates ancestral DNA composition from observable facial features. Measurements are derived from photogrammetric analysis calibrated against the ALFRED (Allele Frequency Database), HapMap Phase III, and the Simons Genome Diversity Project reference datasets. Haplogroup probabilities are estimated using Bayesian posterior inference against known population frequencies in North Africa, the Levant, and South Asia. Confidence intervals (±8-12%) reflect inherent limitations of phenotypic inference.
